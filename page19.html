Decline Crunches

Abs Workout

1

You will need a decline bench that allows you to hook your feet. Position yourself on a decline bench with your feet locked in it. Your upper body should be raised off the bench, so you will have to contract your abs just to stay in place. Place your hands on each side of your head, over your ears. Don't lock your fingers. Raise your upper body slowly while you contract your abs. Crunch up until your elbows are on either side of your thighs. Hold and flex, then lower your body slowly back to the starting position. When your stregth increases and you can do more than 12 reps, hold a weight plate against your chest and perform the excercise in the same manner.
